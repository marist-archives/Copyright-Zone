Copyright Syllabus Statement

To comply with the TEACH Act, students in a course must be notified that “materials used in connection with the course may be subject to copyright protection” (17 USCS § 110). Incorporate a statement, such as the example below, in your course syllabus to provide notification to your students.

Materials used in connection with the course may be subject to copyright protection.
